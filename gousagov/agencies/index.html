Are You Looking for the IRS, Department of Defense or the Social Security Administration?

You may be trying to reach another agency. Below are some tips on connecting with frequently requested agency programs and agency contact information.

Internal Revenue Service - Direct Pay Service

The IRS Direct Pay Service is a secure service to pay your taxes for Form 1040 series, estimated taxes or other associated forms directly from your checking or savings account. Visit IRS Direct Pay to set up an account or for account information, or visit their Get Transcript Services web page for information on requesting a tax transcript or a copy of a previous tax return. If your issue cannot be resolved online, you can call the IRS at 1-800-829-1040.

Department of Defense - Defense Finance and Accounting Service

Retired military & annuitants can connect with the Defense Finance and Accounting Service (DFAS) to access or create a myPay account, or obtain 1099-R forms. Contact the DFAS Customer Service for additional information or assistance.

Department of Defense - TRICARE Program

Visit the TRICARE Program information page to enroll or schedule an appointment with TRICARE service providers or to find the right contact for your needs.

Social Security Administration – my Social Security

Login or create a my Social Security account in order to retrieve a replacement copy of your SSA-1099 or SSA-1042S tax form online. If your issue cannot be resolved online, you can contact the Social Security Administration at 1-800-772-1213 between 7 a.m. and 7 p.m. local time, Monday through Friday. If you are deaf or hard of hearing, call their toll-free TTY number at 1-800-325-0778.
